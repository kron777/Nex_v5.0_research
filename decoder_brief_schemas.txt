  Used for idea-level deduplication — if the same droplet appears >= 2 times in the
  last hour, the crystallizer rejects the crystallization. NULL for early fires (before
  Condenser was introduced) and for quiescent/voice_fallback fires.

--- collision_grades.* ---

Columns: id | belief_id | parent_a_id | parent_b_id | input_distance | output_distance | rarity | grade | grader_version | graded_at

Sample rows (from beliefs_snap.db):
  1 | 2857 | 107 | 272 | 0.3687 | 0.2817 | 1.0    | 0.4961 | 1 | 1777041600.07
  2 | 2934 | 128 | 273 | 0.4523 | 0.2760 | 0.8333 | 0.4859 | 1 | 1777042186.39
  3 | 3088 | 129 | 274 | 0.3859 | 0.2894 | 0.7143 | 0.4342 | 1 | 1777043305.81

belief_id: the synergized child belief.
parent_a_id, parent_b_id: the two input beliefs fed to the synergizer.
input_distance: cosine distance between the two parent embeddings (1 - cosine_sim).
  Higher = parents were more semantically distinct.
output_distance: cosine distance between child embedding and mean-parent embedding.
  Higher = child diverged further from parents.
rarity: inverse rank score — how unusual this child is compared to recent synergized
  beliefs (1.0 = completely novel, 0.0 = duplicate of something recent).
grade: weighted composite (w_input_distance * input_distance + w_output_distance *
  output_distance + w_rarity * rarity). Values in snapshot: 0.43-0.50.
grader_version: integer referencing grader_versions.version.
graded_at: unix float timestamp when grade was computed.
