EVIDENCE:

  1. INVERSE RELATIONSHIP: The bucket with highest spark rate is syn-dominant
     (fi=1-2, syn=5-6): 3 sparks / 24 fires = 12.5%. The bucket with lowest
     (non-zero) spark rate is fi-dominant (fi=5-6, syn=1-2): 2 sparks / 68
     fires = 2.9%. The pure-fountain bucket (0 syn) has 0 sparks.

  2. MONOTONICITY FAILS: Spark rate by fi count: fi=2→18.8%, fi=3→12.0%,
     fi=4→10.8%, fi=5→2.2%, fi=6→4.5%, fi=7→0.0%. The rate declines as
     fountain_insight presence INCREASES. The predicted monotonic rise does
     not appear.

  3. STATISTICS: Sparks have mean fi_ratio 0.486, non-sparks 0.573. Sparks
     lean more syn-dominant than non-sparks, not more fi-dominant. Welch t=-1.38
     (not significant; small n=10) but direction opposes the hypothesis.

  4. INDIVIDUAL SPARKS: 5 of 10 post-restart sparks fire with fi=2 or fi=3
     (syn-heavy context): beliefs 1737, 1739, 3494, 3844 (fi=2, syn=5), and
     3441, 3850 (fi=3, syn=4). Only 2 of 10 fire with fi=5 or fi=6.

  5. PURE-FOUNTAIN PRODUCES NO SPARKS: The 2 fires with all-fountain_insight
     context (fev=247, 262) yielded "Why don't I skip the gym?" / "Why don't
     I watch that new comedy series?" — ACTION opcode, not sparks. High
     fountain_insight presence, zero spark output.

WHAT THE DATA SHOWS INSTEAD:

  The top-7 own-content composition does not predict spark occurrence.
  Spark clusters appear to be driven by:

  (a) TOPICAL ACCUMULATION: Sparks 4196/4199/4277 occur when 6 consecutive
      "clock feels off" fountain_insights have stacked in the slot, providing
      concentrated repetition-detection fodder. Similarly, sparks 3488/3494
      form a mini-cluster when "old journal / rustling leaves" content dominates.
      The spark signal is in the CONTENT of what's in the slot, not the
      source type.

  (b) SEED SLOT: Two random draws from koan/tao/dont_know per fire provide
      the philosophical grounding that enables spark-quality output. The seed
      slot is independent of the own-content composition.

  (c) SENSE INPUT AT FIRE TIME: Hot branch and recent sense sample (3 events)
      determine what external content is injected into the prompt. The quality
      of the sense input (unusual observation vs crypto price update) is a
      stronger predictor of spark output than own-content composition.

  The displacement of introspective fountain_insight by synergized beliefs
  changes the TOPIC priming (from consciousness/silence/paradox to crypto/
  notifications/server hum) but does not directly suppress spark generation.
  Sparks emerge whenever the right sense observation + topical accumulation
  crosses the spark classifier threshold, regardless of synergized presence
  in the own-content slot.
