H1. SIMILE ↔ INTROSPECT (ABSTRACT_NOMINAL) ORTHOGONALITY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

INTROSPECT decryption finding: "essentially orthogonal to SIMILE;
1 ABSTRACT_NOMINAL→SIMILE transition in 61 fires."

Verification from SIMILE side:

  SIMILE fires with ABSTRACT_NOMINAL in ±3 fires:  3/22  (14%)
  Direct adjacency (SIMILE±1 = ABSTRACT_NOMINAL):   1/22  (<5%)

  For contrast:
  SIMILE fires with SENSE_OBS in ±3 fires:         21/22  (95%)

This confirms near-complete orthogonality from the SIMILE side.
SIMILE and ABSTRACT_NOMINAL almost never co-occur within 3 fires.
The 3 exceptions are the 2 non-standard early fires (1169, 1194)
and 1 accidental adjacency in the corpus.

The structural interpretation: SIMILE operates on sensory material,
not abstract material. INTROSPECT operates on the output of reflection,
not sense events. They draw from different input registers and do not
mix. When SIMILE does follow ABSTRACT_NOMINAL (fires 1194, 1169),
the resulting SIMILE is non-standard in format — evidence that the
SIMILE template requires a sense-observation entry condition it is not
getting.
