B. PRIOR 3 FOUNDATION INJECTIONS
======================================================================

NOT MEASURABLE FROM DATABASE.

The generator's _retrieve_context_beliefs() does not log selected belief IDs.
fountain_events contains only: ts, thought, droplet, readiness, hot_branch,
word_count. No per-fire belief tracking exists.

What can be inferred:
  - Seed slot: 2 beliefs drawn ORDER BY RANDOM() from _SEED_SOURCES
    {'koan', 'tao', 'dont_know', 'heart_sutra', 'keystone_seed', ...}
    → On any given fire there is a 2/81 = 2.5% chance of drawing any specific koan.
    With 39 koans and 81 seed-eligible beliefs (pre-syn), expected koans drawn = 0.96/fire.
  - Own-content slot: 7 beliefs drawn by (created_at × boost_value) DESC from
    {'fountain_insight', 'synergized', 'precipitated_from_dynamic', 'behavioural_observation'}
    → In pre-syn era, no synergized available. Own slot draws only fountain_insight
      (recent fires) and precipitated_from_dynamic.
  - Spectrum foundation: 8 random spectrum beliefs injected EVERY fire as "standing-points",
    separate from the belief retrieval pipeline. spectrum (200 beliefs) was created
    AFTER the corpus window — NOT present in any of the 252 corpus fires' substrates.
  - Koan rotation: every 8th fire (fires 7, 15, 23...) one least-recently-read koan
    is injected explicitly at the top of the prompt (requires readiness ≥ 0.8
    AND belief_count ≥ 20).

Summary: for all pre-syn sparks (31/41), the prior context foundation was
koan/tao/dont_know (seed slot) + recent fountain_insight (own slot) + one
explicit koan every 8 fires. Synergized input: zero.

C2. THE CONFOUND

  The koan count is fixed at 39 for ALL 252 fires. The synergized count
  only grows (never shrinks). Therefore koan:synergized = 39/max(syn,1)
  is a monotonically decreasing function of time, not a substrate
  composition measure.

  A Welch t of 5.85 measures the temporal skew of sparks, not koan quality:
    - 31 pre-syn sparks (ratio=39) vs 10 post-syn sparks (ratio<1)
    - 68 pre-syn non-sparks (ratio=39) vs 143 post-syn non-sparks (ratio<1)
  Spark density drops from 31.3% to 6.5% after the synergizer starts.
  This is real, but it is the same finding as Period A/B vs C in
  Phase1_SPARK_conditions.txt — not additional evidence for H3.

C5. VERDICT ON H3

  H3 directionally supported: spark density falls sharply after synergized
  beliefs appear. Koan:synergized ratio correlates with spark probability.

  H3 NOT supported as a direct substrate mechanism:
  1. Koan count is constant — there is no "koan-heavy" substrate state,
     only a "no synergized yet" state. The ratio measures time, not composition.
  2. Within the post-syn era, sparks and non-sparks have nearly identical
     synergized counts (53.2 vs 56.6 mean). The substrate composition does not
     distinguish them.
  3. The 8 post-syn sparks (3441, 3488, 3494, 3844, 3850, 4196, 4199, 4277)
     fire at synergized levels of 39–82 — the same range as non-sparks —
     showing that synergized presence does not block sparks.

  H3 RESTATEMENT AFTER EVIDENCE:
  The synergizer's effect on spark density is likely INDIRECT:
    - Synergizer runs correlate in time with quiescent branch emergence (H2)
    - Synergizer output (resolved, crystallized content) fills the own-content
      pool, potentially competing with novel fountain_insight content for the
      own-content retrieval slot — but there is no direct logging to confirm this
    - The fire-rate slowdown (H1) is the strongest era-differentiator and it
      is independent of substrate composition

  H3 as stated should be revised to H3': "Synergizer era, not synergized
  substrate composition per se, correlates with reduced spark density."
