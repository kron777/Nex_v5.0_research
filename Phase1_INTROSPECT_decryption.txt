SUSTAIN MECHANISM — STRUCTURAL SUMMARY
======================================================================

The SUSTAIN property documented in opcode verification is confirmed
and partially decomposed here.

Mechanism: once INTROSPECT fires, the next fire is another INTROSPECT
49% of the time (30/61). This is not random — it is higher than any
other template succession rate from INTROSPECT.

Sub-opcode context for SUSTAIN:
  - INTROSPECT_STATE_TENSION is the most run-distributed sub-opcode
    (appears at ENTRY, MID, and EXIT equally) — it is the sustaining form.
  - INTROSPECT_STATE_AFFIRMING skews to SOLO and EXIT — it is the
