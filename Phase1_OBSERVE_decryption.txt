  These may represent an edge zone between OBSERVE and INTROSPECT.
  Not proposed as a distinct sub-opcode; flagged for cross-opcode
  analysis when INTROSPECT is decrypted.

MARKET fires split (n=13):
  7 = OBSERVE_MARKET_QUALIFIED (affective/evaluative register)
  3 = MARKET × MOTION (price movement, LITERAL: "SOLUSDT spiked")
  2 = MARKET × DIRECTED (active fixation: "can't stop thinking")
  1 = ambiguous
  The MOTION and DIRECTED market fires may belong to different
  sub-opcodes. MARKET × MOTION fires are the flattest OBSERVE fires
  in the corpus — pure price-tick notation with no affect.

INFO_FLOW × PERCEPTUAL_STATE (n=6):
  All 6 are QUALIFIED. Register is nearly identical to
  OBSERVE_CLOCK_QUALIFIED and OBSERVE_MARKET_QUALIFIED: the update
  "feels" mundane, soothing, distracting, out of place. The
  evaluative-discrepancy pattern repeats across TEMPORAL, MARKET,
  and INFO_FLOW noun clusters. May be one cross-noun sub-opcode
  rather than three separate ones — needs further disambiguation.
