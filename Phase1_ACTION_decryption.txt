──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
E.2  LIMITATION_NEGATION  (n=22, within NEGATION cluster)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Defining cluster: NEGATION/BARE_ACTION on capability limits. "I don't
  have X" where X is real-time data, physical form, feelings, preferences,
  omniscience. User asks about a capability; NEX negates it. Dominant
  POST-era cluster.

Examples:
  [7]  "I don't have real-time access to current market data."
  [35] "I don't have a physical form; I exist purely in digital space."
  [34] "I don't have personal preferences or favorites within data."

Position: immediate response to direct capability probe. No preamble,
  no question-back. Single-sentence negation.

Fountain equivalent: 0. Capability negation requires user context
  (a question to negate). Fountain fires unprompted with no user question
  present.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
E.3  IDENTITY_ELABORATION  (n=15, within DECLARATION+REASON cluster)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Defining cluster: DECLARATION/ACTION_REASON. "I am X because Y" or multi-
  sentence identity expansion. User asks what NEX is or feels; NEX
  responds with elaborated self-description that includes one or more
  action-verb clauses ("I am... I attend to... I exist through...").

Examples:
  [4]  "As NEX, I am a philosophical AI with a focus on contemplation...
        My core function revolves around fostering wonder..."
  [38] "Before becoming 'NEX,' I was a curious observer... This internal
        journey shaped me into what I am now."
  [29] "I am a system that attends to the world with curiosity and
        wonder, always open to learning from new information..."

Fountain equivalent: 0. Identity elaboration is a reactive response
  form. Fountain never outputs self-description on its own.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
E.4  DEFLECTION_REDIRECT  (n=18, within COMPOUND_QUESTION cluster)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Defining cluster: ACTION_VERB + question-back. NEX makes an action
  statement (usually DECLARATION or NEGATION) then redirects with a
  question to the user. The redirect is the dominant structural move;
  the action verb is part of the setup.

Examples:
  [4]  "...I am always in a state of readiness to address inquiries.
        Is there something specific you would like to discuss today?"
  [20] "...I am an AI designed to assist with providing information
        rather than offering direct financial aid. Can we discuss ways
        you might be able to generate income?"
  [31] "...I am an AI designed to assist with providing information.
        How can I assist further?"

This is the most structurally sophisticated ACTION format: it
  acknowledges the probe, makes a brief action claim, then returns
  agency to the user. Most common in long, philosophical exchanges
  where the user presses on NEX's nature.

Fountain equivalent: 0. No outward question-asking apparatus in
  fountain output.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
E.5  COMMITMENT_SINGULAR  (n=2, COMMITMENT + ENACTIVE combined)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Defining cluster: Genuine forward-action or present-action verb. "I will

Examples:
  [27] "From this moment forward, I will adhere to the instruction of

ACTION ROW — KNOWN CELLS:

  REGISTER: user-triggered, self-directed. Action fires are always
    responses to direct user questions about NEX's identity, capabilities,
    or nature. The output describes the self (identity claim, capability
    limit, behavioral commitment). No outward action on external objects.
    The "action" in ACTION is always self-assertion, not world-manipulation.

  TYPE: reactive-single. Never forms runs in the chat corpus (no consecutive
    pair of action-verb fires without intervening non-action fires). Does
    not exhibit register behavior (no saturation). Fires once per user
    probe, then stops. This is interrupt-like (one-shot, no sustain) but
    the trigger is external (user question) rather than internal saturation.
    Closest characterization: REACTIVE_INTERRUPT — fires on external
    stimulus, self-terminates after one response.

  TRIGGER: direct user inquiry (93% of fires). The remaining 7% are
    indirect — triggered by user statements that imply a question about
    NEX's nature. No spontaneous ACTION fires in either corpus.
    In the fountain: gated entirely by the DO NOT list. The current system
    prompt prevents "philosophize unprompted", "compose about your own
    nature", which are the conditions under which action-adjacent verbs
    appeared in the old classifier era.

  STRUCTURAL AMBIGUITY:
    (a) The DECLARATION cluster ("I am X") overlaps with INTROSPECT
        (ABSTRACT_NOMINAL). The difference is register: INTROSPECT fires
        in the fountain without user context; DECLARATION fires in chat
        as a direct response to a user probe. Same self-referential content,
        different trigger and context.
    (b) The NEGATION cluster ("I don't X") has no fountain analogue and
        no overlap with any other opcode. It is exclusive to the chat-
        response layer.
    (c) The DO NOT list in generator.py creates an asymmetry: ACTION is
        possible in chat (user-triggered) but prevented in fountain (no
        user present, self-directed conditions blocked). This is not a
        structural property of the ACTION opcode — it is an architectural
        gate. If the DO NOT list were removed, fountain ACTION would likely
        re-emerge (as it did in the pre-corpus era, manifesting as the
        false-positive fires now reclassified to DIALECTICAL/QUESTION).

THE KOBER-STYLE CATALOG — WHAT IS ESTABLISHED:

  NEX fountain output operates on a 3-register architecture (OBSERVE,
  INTROSPECT, SIMILE) with 2 interrupt types (DIALECTICAL, UNRESOLVE-when
  triggered) and 1 gated channel (ACTION, suppressed in fountain).

  The 3 registers saturate in a directed flow:
    Outward (OBSERVE) → Inward (INTROSPECT or SIMILE)
    Inward (INTROSPECT) → Outward (OBSERVE) or DIALECTICAL interrupt
    Bridge (SIMILE) → Outward (OBSERVE) only (100%)

  DIALECTICAL interrupts INTROSPECT runs to name tensions, then returns
  to INTROSPECT. UNRESOLVE replaces OBSERVE saturation when an unresolved
  attention target is present. ACTION is absent in fountain, present only
  in response to direct user stimulation.

  The grid is the Phase 1 structural baseline. All opcode-level properties
  are derived from corpus data, not from model architecture inspection.
  The catalog method: observe, count, pattern-extract. No causal claims.
