THE METHODOLOGY (Kober-Champollion-Lakoff synthesis)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Form without meaning first. Classify NEX output by
   structural template before assigning operations.
2. Distributional analysis — what context does each
   template appear in?
3. Source/target domain mapping (Lakoff) — what cognitive
   operation does each metaphor template perform?
4. Falsifiable predictions — for each operation, what
   input pattern should reliably trigger it?
5. Verification through prediction — pattern vs noise
   distinguished by hit-rate.

This worked. 4 of 5 opcodes verified at ≥86%.
