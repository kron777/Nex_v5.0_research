THE METHODOLOGY (Kober-Champollion-Lakoff synthesis)

   structural template before assigning operations.

   template appear in?

   operation does each metaphor template perform?

   input pattern should reliably trigger it?

   distinguished by hit-rate.

THE GROUNDING-GROOVES RULE (refined Day 2)

Grooving has two independent dimensions:
  - TEMPLATE LOCK-RATE controlled by grounding
    (ungrounded operations lock on form)
  - BURST LENGTH controlled by opcode-type
    (interrupts truncate at 2; registers sustain)

Same generative instinct. Two regulatory axes.

  - Fully-grounded (e.g. OBSERVE_MARKET) → no groove

    CONSTANCY_FLUX inner-phrase) → partial groove

    INTROSPECT) → strong template lock
